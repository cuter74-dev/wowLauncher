# Privacy Policy — wowLauncher

_Last updated: 2026-06-26_

wowLauncher ("the app") is a remote launcher that lets your phone launch
programs **pre-registered on your own PC** over your local Wi‑Fi network. We
designed it to keep your data on your own devices.

## What we collect

**We do not collect personal information.** The app has no account system and
does not ask for your name, email, contacts, photos, or location.

Data the app stores **only on your device**:

- PC connection details you add (PC name, local IP address, port).
- The access token issued by your PC during pairing.

This information never leaves your device except to communicate with the PC you
paired with, on your local network.

## Network communication

- The app communicates **only with the PC (desktop agent) you paired with**,
  over your local Wi‑Fi/LAN, using the address you configured.
- The app does not send your data to us or to any third‑party advertising or
  analytics service for profiling.

## Crash & error diagnostics

To keep the app stable, the app may send **anonymous crash and error reports**
to a self‑hosted GlitchTip (Sentry‑compatible) instance operated by the
developer. These reports contain technical information such as the error
message, a stack trace, the app version, and the device model/OS version. They
**do not contain** your PC connection details, access tokens, or any personal
identifiers. Diagnostics are disabled in builds without a configured reporting
endpoint.

## Camera

The app uses the camera **only** to scan the pairing QR code shown on your PC.
Camera images are processed on‑device and are not stored or transmitted.

## Local network

The app requests Local Network access **only** to connect to your PC on the
same Wi‑Fi network.

## Data retention & deletion

All app data is stored locally. You can delete it at any time by removing a PC
from the list or uninstalling the app.

## Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from
children.

## Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be posted on this page.
